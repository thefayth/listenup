## Hero

ListenUp is a transcription and caption studio for clean, searchable media workflows.

Alt text: ListenUp Studio Console showing a clean transcript workflow with waveform preview.

## What This Project Is

ListenUp helps turn owned media, direct media URLs, and pasted captions into clean transcripts that can be searched, saved, edited, and exported.

## Why It Matters

Transcripts are not just raw text. They become captions, public records, notes, accessibility tools, research material, content assets, and receipts. ListenUp is designed for the practical workflow between media and usable text.

## How It Works

1. Bring in owned media, a direct media URL, or existing captions.
2. Use the Studio Console to review and clean transcript output.
3. Save transcript history behind an account.
4. Export TXT, SRT, or VTT files.
