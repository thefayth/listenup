# FAQ

## Is this the source code?

No. This is a protected public project surface. It is not the full source code, operational system, private workflow, or data room.

## Can I reuse the project?

No permission is granted for copying, redistribution, training, commercial reuse, or derivative works.

## What does ListenUp do?

ListenUp turns owned media, direct media URLs, and pasted captions into cleaner searchable transcript workflows with export-ready TXT, SRT, and VTT outputs.

## Why not publish the engine?

The source code, prompts, agent workflows, data handling, deployment systems, credentials, and operational infrastructure are private intellectual property.

## Is ListenUp deployed?

The private app is locally verified. Public deployment is a separate private operational step.
